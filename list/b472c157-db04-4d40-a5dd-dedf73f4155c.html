About the Game



Simple to play, as the new generation of digital card games, and strategic as traditional ones. Discover the world of ManaRocks and its unique features:

  • Seasonal Card Game
    On SCGs, the set of cards changes every season, creating a new experience and always changing meta for the players. Everyone starts each season with the same basic cards and unlocks the remaining through playing and evolving. A real Free to Play game, no Booster Packs no Loot Boxes.

  • 2v2 Co-Op Game Mode
    You can play with your friend or with a random partner in epic matches. Use the same battlefield with your partner and explore your creativity to create synergies and combos between your decks.

  • New Rewards Each Season
    Earn new customize and collectibles rewards each Season. New Heroes, card backs, emotes and more!
