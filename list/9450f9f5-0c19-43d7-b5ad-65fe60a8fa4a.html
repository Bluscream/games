Description

CyberMotion Games presents you the online role-playing game Block Multiplayer RPG, which will certainly fascinate all the players! The new project will combine a favorite and familiar RPG gameplay with notes of unique innovations.

To rob a bank or a liquor store? To go to the beach away from all affairs? Complete freedom of action! A unique level system will make your character stronger and better with each new criminal case. Pump up power, endurance, shooting skills, outrun rivals and become the best!

Don't you like to do underground work? You have the opportunity to go to work peacefully. In this game, there is a place and a necessary role for everyone.

The servers will contain the right number of people to assemble a gang of reliable friends and declare war on local crime, which have managed to ‘do a bad turn’ to you.

The process of a gang war and the acquisition of territories are given sufficient attention. Start with a simple pawn, climb to the top of the pyramid of power. The more senior your rank, the greater the share!

Fans of fashionable characters will appreciate an extensive collection of clothing, accessories, and hairstyles. Everyone will find a unique combination for any taste and style.

Real estate will also delight you with its diversity. Whether you want to live in the center or on the outskirts, you can find a cozy house or luxurious apartments, there is enough to go round!

Racers always have a variety of cars. Aren’t you satisfied with the specifications or color? The tuning house will easily remedy this situation, and your own garage will ensure the safety of expensive cars that are pumped to the maximum level.

Businessmen can open their business on the accrued capital, multiplying it in the bar, club or office. In this city, there are many opportunities to try your hand at different business areas.

What about the graphics? Here it will delight even the most demanding judges of quality pictures. Beautiful natural landscapes, stylish architectural solutions, impressive city panorama. All of this creates a unique atmosphere!

Block MP: RPG Features:
• Dynamic multiplayer for 50 players
• Character upgrade
• Heists
• Faction system: Police against gangsters
• Gang zone capture
• Full character customization
• Property buying
• A variety of modes of transport: from motorcycles to airplanes
• A variety of weapons
• Realistic simulation of driving and shooting
• Vehicle modifications
• Business system
