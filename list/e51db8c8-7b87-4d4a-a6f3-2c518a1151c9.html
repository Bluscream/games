Description

Inspired by The Library of Babel by Jorge Luis Borges, the Infinite Art Museum is an endless collection of art. Our mission is to create a growing and enduring home for art in VR that is accessible to everyone.

Explore paintings, illustrations, digital art and photography from creators all over the world with more joining every week.

Visit the Infinite Art Museum website to learn more about our artists or join the IAM.
