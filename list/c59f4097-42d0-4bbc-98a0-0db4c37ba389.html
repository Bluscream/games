About the Game

Fight for freedom of people in this dictature simulation!
Use different skills to make the revolt gauge grow!
Don't get caught by submitting yourself to guards' abuse of authority!

Paint graffitis

Images are the best propaganda. Paint graffitis on walls, make them bigger to gain more revolt points.

Destroy power figures

The icons of the dictature keeps it in position of power. Use a hammer to break them and help people rise.

Stick April Fool on the back of state guards

Humour is devastating against oppression. Stick April fool on the back of the guards to make them look ridiculous.

Without being caught and sent to jail!

Abuse of authority, stupid orders, surveillance, are all dictature weapons to submit people. Be careful to stay discreet in order to prevent you from jail.
