About the Game

A ball rolling game has never been darker.



Dark Roll is a FREE single player platform game, in which the player goes through a series of uniquely designed levels. Your agility will be tested along with your logic. Each level is planned to provide a different challenge. The further you'll get, the more you'll be surprised with what the game has to offer.

Pick one of many skins, even color it if you'd like, and roll your way through various worlds while taking on challenging levels. Collect gems, solve puzzles and face enemies, before finding the exit. Test yourself trying to obtain Steam achievements designed to extend the challenge that Dark Roll offers itself. Or play the Endless Roll mode, where you keep rolling on a procedurally generated road to get the best possible score on Steam Leaderboards.

FEATURES

  • Simple to understand gameplay mechanics
  • Demanding level of difficulty
  • Baby Mode difficulty setting for players who prefer it the easy way
  • 3 worlds different not only in visual style but also in challenges that they provide
  • 20 uniquely designed levels
  • 24 skins to choose from for your ball, 12 of them you can colour to your liking
  • 17 more skins that you can unlock
  • Steam achievements designed to extend the gameplay and provide additional challenges
  • Endless Roll - a bonus game mode with Steam Leaderboards - roll on for as long as you can on a procedurally generated road
