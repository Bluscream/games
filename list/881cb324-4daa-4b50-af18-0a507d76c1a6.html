About the Game

/!\ THE GAME IS STILL UNDER DEVELOPMENT, THE ACTUAL VERSION IS AN ALPHA, WE WILL CONTINUE TO UPDATE AS MUCH AS WE CAN /!\

After several years of war, peace finally reigned over the world of Rhea. The Elves, Humans and Orcs live in harmony, and focus on rebuilding their realms. Nevertheless, a veil of shadow stands over Rhea. The past tries to resurface and appropriate the future of the earth. Will you be able to prevent the change of history? Will you manage to save the life that Rhea people?


GAMEPLAY
Kingdom of Rhea is an Action-RPG, Die & Retry. Monsters more powerful than each other will try to block you in your quest, only the bravest adventurers will be able to face them. During your journey, you will be able to choose an affiliation to a deity, and it will give you a blessing granting you new characteristics and skills to face all your future dangers.


THE FATE OF RHEA
Several years ago, the Eluhims inhabited Rhea. They represent the gods of this world, powerful beings with extraordinary magical powers. Belligerent and proud, the Eluhims made war all the time. But one day, one of the five clans of the Eluhims, decided to banish all the Eluhims from this world in order to preserve Rhea for the new emerging species. However, some Gods have managed to resist, and some of them threaten today’s world.
