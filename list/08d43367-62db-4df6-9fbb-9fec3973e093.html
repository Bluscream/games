A NOTE FROM THE CREATOR

Hello. I am the Creator. I created this Visual Novel's script, code, and music.

The game runs in the RenPy engine and is formatted as a visual novel. Occasionally you will choose what to say by selecting a dialogue box. There are multiple endings. It takes around 2-3 hours to complete everything.

It is recommended that players are at least 12 years or older. The game contains strong language and the occasional discussion of heavy topics. There is no shock imagery or jump scares.

The complete soundtrack is also available. It contains a little over an hour of music.
Thank you to SparkBag, Keeby, and Alexander A. McDonald for creating the majority of the visual assets.

Please enjoy free of charge.

-The Creator.
