Description

Introduction
"Heroes of Fantasia" is a roguelike adventure game. There are many different scenes and maps to explore. In the ARENA, you can release your anger. In the SKYCLOUD, you can be the top of the list. In the world boss battle, you can be the brightest star in the night sky! Do you want to know who the demon hunter is? How other players’ performance in the cross server boss war? Who is the king of warriors? In the cross server segment match, let the world know who will be the top list.

Random maps and Monsters:
Hundreds of levels, hundreds of monsters and elite monsters, and dozens of boss. More scenery to see. If you die in the adventure, there is basically no punishment.

Adventure system:
You may encounter a variety of adventure systems and surprises on the way to explore

Characteristic skills:
Once the super powerful active skills and passive skills play their power, they will be unstoppable. Only reasonable combination can give full play to your combat ability.

Equipment:
A set of equipment is changed every 20 levels in the game, and the attributes of equipment are totally different every. More equipment is waiting for you.

Strong suit:
If you don't think the common dropped equipment can bring you strong combat power, you can try to accumulate a suit. It will bring you a totally different experience.

Spirit guide:
By eliminating more demons, you can get their seals. You will be surprised to find that there are some special fetters between them. If you collect them, you will get the fighting skills of demons.

Rune:
The Magic Runes have strong magic power, you need to compete fairly with other warriors to get it. The power of artifact is only available to warriors who challenge.

Four elves:
The four elves of water, fire, light and dark have their own strengths. They are not only the protagonists, but also powerful fighting partners. There are nine different abilities waiting for you to unlock.
