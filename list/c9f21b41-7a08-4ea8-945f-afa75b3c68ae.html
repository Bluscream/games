About the Game

GRO... TO... RO... GROTORO!!!

GROTORO is a multiplayer couch space globular fatty nervous farting bull brawl in space.


Reflex, timing, precision, courage, endurance, tactics, feints, coalitions, treachery, charisma, tenacity, manipulation, power... Here are all the qualities you must have to be the greasiest Grotoro!

Features:

  • 4 players epic battles!
  • Play with AI if you don't have enough friends
  • Speed control Blobby Volley style: from 50% to 200%
  • Fully editable controls
  • Use the "Yaaa" (dash) to hurt and kill your opponents
  • Master the "Olé" to dodge opponents attacks...
  • Bounce on screen borders to hit them with style
  • Hide behind your fat balls to trick other players
  • Dash into others Grotoros' dash to perform an epic counter
  • Bouncy interface
  • Instant fun: 0 tuto, 0 story, 0 loading
  • Be the last and greasiest Grotoro!
