Description



In this action-packed arcade shooter, build the arena and then fight your opponents by calculating the bounces of your shots, manipulating the map, and dashing to safety!

In Corridor Crusaders, the PvP matches consist of a phase of building, where both players take turns placing tetris-like pieces on a grid to form the walls. Then, both players spawn on their map and battle each other in Arena-style combat. Pick your weapon, line up your shots, use rockets to destroy a wall, or dash around a corner to gain the advantage and eliminate your opponents.

Disclaimer

The game is also only playable in online mode, so there must be a player queuing at the same time as you to enter a match.
