About the Game

Force of Elements is the game of head-to-head combat where your field of battle is a match 3 gem board. Choose a unique hero, swap gems to unlock elemental mana and annihilate your opponent in fast-paced battles that will leave you hungry for one more round.

Less candy, More CRUSH.

Force of Elements is designed from the ground up for competitive gaming with diverse character-based abilities that make interacting with the gem board a tactical experience unlike any match 3 game you’ve played before. Anyone can learn to play in minutes. Mastering your favorite heroes and outwitting opponents in real-time battles will keep you playing and streaming for years to come.

Key Features:

  • Two ways to play; rapid-fire TIMED mode and tactical TURNS mode
  • Customize your play style with powerful AURAS
  • NEW HEROES released regularly
  • RANKED play and TOURNAMENTS - COMING SOON

Unleash the POWER of the ELEMENTS!

In the realm of Acharos, power derives from the five elements. Each Hero has an affinity with their chosen element, but skillful players can tap into all five by creating power gems.

  • Fire: Incinerate your enemies with primordial energy. Fire is the element of chaos and destruction.
  • Air: Masters of Air can manipulate time itself, extending their turns for bigger combos. Knowledge and control are attributes of Air.
  • Water: The most protean element, Water is all about transformation. Water heroes manipulate the gem board to their advantage.
  • Earth: Call on the forces of nature to outlast your opponent. Earth abilities emphasize strength and renewal.
  • Void: The essence of emptiness, Void is a dangerous element to embrace. Void abilities allow heroes to curse, cripple and drain their opponents…often at a price.
