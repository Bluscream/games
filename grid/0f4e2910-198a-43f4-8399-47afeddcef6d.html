Description

You thought you were the only rock climbing hipster around, but these mountains are full of them. Eliminate all competition and claim your rock!
Climb, an online multiplayer brawler with short and action packed game sessions!

We are releasing Climb to the public to see if a fast paced deathmatch game is what the players want (also because it is fun to play!).
We seek your feedback and wishes for upcoming features such as:
  • Mechanics
  • Levels
  • Steam functions (workshop, leaderboards, achievements, etc)
  • Customization items etc
