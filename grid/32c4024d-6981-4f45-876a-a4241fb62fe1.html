Description

Escape from prison and swing your way through the city, avoid various obstacles, don't get caught and try not to fall.
Compete with friends and rise to the top of the leaderboard!

VR Fitness Insider review: 4/5

Features:
  • Realistic swinging-physics
  • Global leaderboard & personal statistics
  • Achievements
  • Frequent updates
  • Randomly generated environment every new run!
  • Comfort settings ("anti-motion sickness")

Bonus:
  • Humor & easter eggs
  • Feel (and look) like Tarzan
  • Sore arms

Planned features:
  • Multiplayer mode! - in the works
  • Cosmetic appearance
  • Multiple environments

Escape, swing, fall, repeat.
