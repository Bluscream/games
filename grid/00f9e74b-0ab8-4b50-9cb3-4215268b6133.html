Description

Fight intense 1-on-1 battles with your friends!
Kra-Ken is a 2.5D fighting game that will make you grip your controller ever more tightly as you beat your opponent.

Kra-Ken aims to emulate our own view of the Street Fighter legacy and pay homage to the many years of inspiration and joy it has brought.
Experience the two unique characters and stages as envisioned by DGTL Dojo, a team of passionate students from the Netherlands.

Features

  • 2 Unique Characters
    Fight as Fabio, the handsome, mustache wielding brawler! Or Dehlila, the beautiful and quick dancer!
  • Local Multiplayer
    Play with your friends on the same machine!
  • VS AI gameplay
    Practice and hone your skills against the AI.

    If you intend to play the game with a keyboard, the controls are as follows:
    Movement with WASD.
    Attacking with UIOJKL.
