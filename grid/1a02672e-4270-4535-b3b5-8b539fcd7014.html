CATS!



CATS! is a CAT-based parody of some of the top video games of ALL time -
Enjoy parodies inspired by Super Mario Bros®, Tetris™, Candy Crush Saga®, Nintendogs + Cats™, Sonic the Hedgehog® + more - all from a Cat's perspective!

The game also features a "Pet Toy" mode so that your pet can interact with the game. Just leave your pet alone with your screen on and let your pet try to chase and catch various objects on screen.



----------

CATS! currently features the following 5 modes:

#1. Arcade mode - side-scrolling Super Mario Bros. and Sonic the Hedgehog -inspired mode starring CATS!

#2. Puzzle mode - Tetris and Candy Crush Saga -inspired mode starring CATS!

#3. Pet Toy mode - your pet (cat or dog) can interact with the game in real-time! Just leave your pet alone with your screen on and let your pet try to chase and catch various objects on screen.

#4. Jump mode - Sonic the Hedgehog and Super Mario Bros -inspired mode starring CATS!

#5. Screensaver mode - Nintendogs + Cats -inspired mode starring CATS!

We plan to add more levels and characters to the game...
