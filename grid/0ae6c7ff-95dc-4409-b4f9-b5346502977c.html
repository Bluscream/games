ABOUT THIS GAME

Save the city of Ivory Skyline from Company Corp, an evil corporation hell bent on draining the color and life out of everything!

Skate, grind, wall ride, and kick off drones to explore an open-world, stylized environment. Then, become graffiti and bring your environment to life. Unlock new abilities to move around the city fluidly, and use these abilities to take down Company Corp once and for all.

Chromalition is a third-person action platformer where you play as a graffiti artist who can become graffiti and bring graffiti to life. Find and complete graffiti murals around the city, unlock new abilities, and take down Company Corp!

KEY FEATURES

  • Flatten and turn 2D to bring graffiti in your environment to life


  • Skate, grind, wall ride, and kick off drones to move around the city

  • Explore an open-world city
  • Complete missions to unlock new skins
