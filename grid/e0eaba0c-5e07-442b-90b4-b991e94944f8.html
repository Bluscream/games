Description

Think of Downtown Drift as pac-man, but as a first-person racing game. You need to try and score as many points as possible by drifting while police is chasing you down. Each drift gives you a certain amount of points and also increases your multiplier. The higher the multiplier, the more points you get for each drift. However there is a catch: you only have limited health and you take damage everytime you crash, so watch how you move!

Features


  • Fast pace racing with thrilling police chases
  • Realistic car destruction
  • Global Highscore Ranking to compete with friends and players around the world
