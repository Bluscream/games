About the Game

JOIN OUR DISCORD




Confessions is a free chapter of Mayhem in Single Valley. Don't forget to add the full game to your wishlist!

Mayhem in Single Valley is an action-adventure full of twists and radioactive squirrels. This is the first day of the Apocalypse in a small town - and it's all because of a young boy named Jack. Mix monster feeding with your housework and zombie assault with your family dinner. Welcome to Single Valley!

Living in a small town is hard. Especially when you have family problems, crazed animals ate your neighbors and everybody blames you for the apocalypse. This is how the day starts for Jack, not so lucky guy.

A Weird World with Unexpected Events: Single Valley is a place where you never know what’s next. Rafting on the poisoned river and Elementary School of Ghosts. Physical puzzles and car chases. Self-cloning, dad’s disapproval, and vice-versa. You will be surprised.



The Strangest Story: Jack has to deal not only with the Apocalypse but also with his inner world. Fourth wall breaking discoveries, family problems, and suspicious inconsistencies in your past. What are you hiding, mister Jack?



Lots of Creatures, Enemies, and Bad Things: Enemies have preferences, behavior, and character. Don’t kill: improvise! If super-radioactive bunnies still love carrots, maybe the toxic bear will find something tastier than you? We have a full radioactive Zoo here! No thanks necessary.

See you in Single Valley!
