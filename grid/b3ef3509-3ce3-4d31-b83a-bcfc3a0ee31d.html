WHAT IS MAZE QORE ARENA?



Maze Qore Arena is a top-down, bullet hell, action-arena shooter of wonders you never heard of! Bundled with a wide variety of multiplayer levels of bullet-raining hell, a slew of multiplayer game modes, and the ability to customize the world!

CUSTOMIZE YOUR MANNÉ, WEAPONS, AND WORLD



  • Customize your mannequin using the Sprite Editor! Use various presets ranging from your average manné to aliens, to superheroes, to zombies! Or import your own custom sprites!

  • You can also customize weapon and item presets to customize your world as you see fit! Choose from a variety of theme presets like war, sci-fi, horror, steampunk, cyberpunk, and more! You can also import your own custom themes!

  • Create your own custom levels in the Labyrinth! Use a variety of tilesets from the standard sector colors or custom tilesets! Using the custom tileset mode, you can choose from island, city, villa, war zone, or import your own custom tilesets!

GAME MODES



  • Riot - Kill every other mannequin on sight! Score the most kills to win!
  • Gang Riot - Gang up with friends and defeat the enemy gang! Gang with the most kills are the victor!
  • Flag Hustle - Steal the enemy gang's flag and bring it back home! Capture the most flags or reach the goal to win!
  • Jail Bait - You're the bait! Capture the area and defend it for as long as possible until the time runs out! Gang with the most defense time wins!
  • Identity Theft - Steal the ID card, escape the area or kill all the other players to win! Player with the fastest completion time wins!
  • Rendezvous - Offense team must escort a mobile objective (vehicle or person) to the goal point, destroying any barriers along the way. Defense team must prevent the Offense team from completing their objective until the time runs out.
  • All Out War - Invade the enemy gang's turf and wipe them all out, or detonate a bomb in their turf. Gang with the fastest completion time wins!

EARLY ACCESS



This game is currently in early-access so not all features are completed. We plan to have a fully-functional multiplayer experience that will definitely live up to the game's title "Arena", complete with achievements and our own custom level editor. By supporting us, we can make such goal possible in a much faster time-span.
