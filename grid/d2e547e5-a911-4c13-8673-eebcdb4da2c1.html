Description

(Thanks player Nephilim(NA Server) gave good editing for our game introduction)

"Eternal Battlefield" is a multiplayer war game


Take charge of hundreds of unique soldiers and powerful heroic leaders to destroy enemy players in a fast-paced eight minute long arena battle.
The game simplifies unit management and resource allocation of classic real-time strategy games, while keeping the excitement and fun of building an army and leading it into battle.
All game content is accessible without the purchase of premium currency, which only provides a faster way to progress.

Game Play


Assemble your army:

◆ Choose from three different races: Undead Elves and Humans.
◆ Create an army loadout comprised of a hero and six units.
◆ Each race, hero and unit comes with their own strategies. Find the one that suits your playstyle!



Crush your enemies:

◆ Command dozens of mages to decimate the playing field with fireballs, or lead a heroic charge with a contingent of knights; choosing the right strategy could mean the difference between overwhelming victory or crushing defeat!
◆ Tactically use your unit and hero skills. Careful, they have cooldowns!



Those who play together win together:

◆ As a team game, cooperation and communication are key.
◆ Separate to optimize your farming and capture neutral objectives, but unite to take down powerful foes!

Game Content


◆ For each of the three races, you get a hero and six powerful units for FREE.
◆ All content can be unlocked permanently using currency obtained from matches.
◆ The remaining heroes and units may be purchased using both free and premium currency.
◆ Use scrolls, gems or free gold to pull stronger units from the summoning stones. Base units are not upgraded, only commanders!

Update plan


We are proud of our game so far, but as we continue to work on balance, game optimization and new content, your game experience may be impacted; we hope you understand. Your suggestions are very important to us as we strive to make the game as fun as possible.
We ask for your patience, however, as we are a small development team and it takes time for us to create new features and push meaningful changes. We will try our best to bring you new updates as soon as possible and hope to have your continued support.
