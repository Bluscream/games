Description

Bomb Bots Arena is the ultimate multiplayer bomb brawler experience!


Join the fierce online competition and be the last Bomb Bot standing in matches with up to 32 players.
And yes, this means the game has a battle royale mode.



Relive the good ol' days of local multiplayer and see the agony in your frenemies' faces when you blast them away.


Create your very own Bomb Bot by unlocking more and more parts while you progress and select your favorite loadout before heading into the Arena and find your favorite playstyle with the following abilites:
  • Kick bombs across the Arena and make them stop just at the right spot to trap and stun your opponents.
  • Shield yourself of any harm for a brief moment but beware: The timing has to be perfect.
  • Build crates to block the enemy's path and to find great loot.
  • Jump over Bombs and other obstacles to escape deadly traps or to pressure your opponents by getting too close too fast.
  • Trigger your Bomb when you think it makes the most sense.
  • Remote Control a pesky little Bomb and chase after your rivals.
  • Punch and dash across the arena to stun your enemies.


  • Free 2 play but not Pay 2 Win.
  • Crossplay with your friends no matter on what they play.
