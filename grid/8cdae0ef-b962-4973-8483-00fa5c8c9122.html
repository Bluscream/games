[Story]

In 2019, one month after the incident of Nadatara mura.
Bando who succeeded in escaping from the village was confined at site X-3 of the foundation / Japan branch.

However, due to the efforts of Captain Albert he will be released and the assignment to a new unit will be decided.

At the same time he was told by the captain that the circumstances surrounding the Foundation had changed significantly.


What is the identity of "Ryouzan kanata"?

Grudge of "Sarkic Cults".

The mystery of "Project MA13".


All stories begin again ......


[Description]

"器関ノ彷徨 -The will of a single Tale-" is a story told in the world view of the SCP Foundation.
