Description

Welcome to edataconsulting offices!
We have the pleasure to invite you to take a virtual visit to our office at Las Palmas de Gran Canaria. Take your VR device and wander around our second home.
We have modeled our facilities for you to learn a bit more about us and get to know a bit about the projects we carry out.

- Move around freely using the controllers from your VR device to teleport or walk around naturally if you have a big room scale available.
- Enjoy the wonderful sights of the port from our city and discover an experience very close to reality with some hidden Easter Eggs.
- Interact with the environment, see what we are working on by checking our computers or take notes with the markers on the whiteboards.
- Reach the meeting room to play a video of our AR space planner tool on the big screen. Also, use the computer in the VR area to experience our project Indus-VR. Yes! VR inside of VR.
- Find the playroom and use physics to interact with the environment.

Don't worry, there is a little tutorial at the beginning and even if you are new to VR experiences, you will get the grip of everything very quickly.

If you like this experience and also want to show your products or projects in VR to your clients, contact us!
