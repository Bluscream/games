Description


PUZZLE: ULTIMATE - a logic game, the task of which is to assemble puzzles of various sizes.

Features:

  • Simple controls (mouse)
  • 30 free levels and a large number of constantly replenishing DLCs (free and paid)
  • Relaxing soundtrack.
  • Beautiful images.
  • Gameplay for all ages.
  • Ability to select a number of puzzles (in the settings menu) from 25 to 225.
