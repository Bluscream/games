Description

Impulse Control is a projectile launching game that features realistic building destruction and assigns you a personality type at the end. Meet the warden of Polar Labs and his sidekick, Marlin. Journey through the 4 elements, answering questions about yourself. Knock down the unique structures in each level with well-placed shots, harmonic resonance, or brute force to create havoc and squash the meanies.

Key features:
  • Realistic structure types featuring columns, frames, trusses and beams
  • challenging and satisfying building destruction!
  • 12 unique personality types with original art and descriptions
  • 28 different levels with unique meanies to squash
  • Free, with no ads or in-app purchases
  • 15-20 minute playthrough with relaxing music

This game is not intended to treat any disorders. Impulse control is something we can all practice. "For every minute we remain angry, we give up 60 seconds of peace of mind" so don't be a meanie, give impulse control a try.
