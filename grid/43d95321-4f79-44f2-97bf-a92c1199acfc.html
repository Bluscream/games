About the Game



About the game Go on a trip to Garlock Online. Collect points, fight numerous monsters, and gain power throughout the game. In Garlock you can choose between different character types and improve them with items as well as armor during the game. Each character has its own level system. Keep your characters in good health to ensure their longevity and help them climb the dungeons together. Rise in level and become stronger than your opponent battles against other players in Epic PVP battles. Garlock Online is a PvP game but also a Singlplayer was integrated with.
