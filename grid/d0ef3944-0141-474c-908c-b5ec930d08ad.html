Description

Kicks Online Freestyle Football is a P2P game available for free on Steam. It featured street soccer modes and rules, 3v3, 4v4, and 5v5 matches, and unconventional fields like roofs, streets and factories.

* Game Language supports:
(English , Spanish , French , Italian , Chinese , Korean)

*Host:
P2P Host , anybody can create their own room using their network , it's recommendable to play with players from your same region.

* Kicks Market:
This is a global market for Kicks Online , players can use the kicks market for:
1. Sell Event Points and get real money ($5.00 USD)
2. Exchange your characters with other players
3. Sell your characters for KASH
4. Buy characters for KASH

More information about the Kicks Market:
https://kicks-online.net/en/market_tos

* Modes rewarded with experience:
Modes: 3vs3 - 4vs4 - 5vs5
Other modes are available but does not reward with experience for level up.

* Beginner players:
The new characters starting from level (1) will receive an special uniform set for (7) days with:
Stats: +10 Agility
Stats: +10 Ball Control

New players also receives experience (*20) until reaching level 18.

At level 18 you choose your final position for your character, more information about positions:
https://kicks-online.net/en/positions

* Ranking system TOP 50 players:
Filtered out by (Highest level - Tackles - Steals - Intercepts , Goals , Assists)

* Items Shop:
Players can buy clothes , items and abilities for their characters - more than 300 new items added for Female/Male characters.

Kicks Online has a global ranking of each player on the official website:
https://kicks-online.net/en/rank

* Rewards feature:
A variety of rewards available!

1. Players that reach level (25) will automatically receive a free backpack for (30) days added to the inventory.
Stats: +10 Agility

2. Reaching level (30) you receive: 1000 KASH

3. Reaching level max (60) you receive: 5000 KASH + Legend Tag

* Club System:
Players can create their own club from the userpanel , buy a primary and secondary uniforms and play on the Club Server which gives the double experience than the normal server.

For more information about the Kicks Online Server on real time:
https://kicks-online.net/en/servers

* Golden Time:
Golden time is an feature that when is enabled, players in-game receive more experience in all matches modes (3vs3,4vs4,5vs5) , also the missions gets better reward than usual.

* Match Log:
Each match completed on Kicks Online is saved and logged on the Match Log Historial of the official website of Kicks Online:
https://kicks-online.net/en/match_log

* Variety of commands added:
#timeleft - room number
#who host - room number
#progress - show the exp left for reach next level
#observer - enable/disable the observer mode
#rewards - display the rewards system

Type #commands to display all the available commands.


* Staff active & support available:
If you ever need assistance with the game itself , your acocunt , or anything related to Kicks Online, we have an advanced support system in the official website.
Log in your account & Open a ticket query , the staff will get back to you as soon as possible.
