About the Software

Free Fluid Engine PC Live Wallpaper will change your wallpaper into an amazing fire spectacle. In the Free version, you can add a clock, logotype and your own text. Now you can set your screen on fire!

Wallpaper for WINDOWS10 ONLY!

Main features:
Live wallpaper - displayed as your wallpaper.
Interactive simulation - Fluid that reacts to your mouse.
Clock - adjust your clock to the screen.
Own text - add your own text to the wallpaper.
Own logo - add your own logotype (.PNG, .JPG)
Autorun - Starts with Windows10.
Automatic and manual Pause Mode - will pause automatically when you play normal games.
Optimization - reduced equipment requirements. Optimized code created for mobile devices.
Multi-Language - the menu with many languages.
24h support - We are here to help with any issue.

Wallpaper for WINDOWS10 ONLY!
