Description

Welcome to Catch the Ball 3! This is an exciting, action-packed skill game where every action you take will help you complete the level or make things difficult. Each move matters and you need to do everything you can in order to finish the level quickly!

How can you play?

The way you play Catch the Ball 3 is very easy. All you have to do is to pick the desired level and put the ball in a trophy. In order to do that you will have to draw lines on the board as you guide that ball to the trophy and not away from it. If the ball gets out of the game board, you will have to try it again.

Acquire as many stars as possible

Every level has 3 stars in total, and you will have to try and acquire all of them if possible. With amazing 50 levels to play through, there’s no shortage of content for you to enjoy!

If you want to test your abilities and speed, then check out Catch the Ball 3 today! This immersive, fun game brings you great ways to refine your strategic approach and skills with every level!

Features:

• 50 levels to play through
• Test your agility and speed
• Refine your strategies
• Lots of replay value
