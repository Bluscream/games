Description

Dive into Drainlive: a PVP Action RPG where skill is the key to victory. With a unique gameplay and experience, very different from its peers, focused on frenetic battles and a very well developed PVP to bring out your best talent, choose your champion, team up with friends, and conquer the Arena.

CHOOSE YOUR CHAMPION

Enter the competition with the champion of your choice, you can go with a Mallet, your daggers, throw arrows or use Magic to defeat your enemies. There are several champions available, but this number does not stop, more and more new ones are coming to join the battle.

COMPETE WITH FRIENDS

Teamwork is crucial in several game modes, Arenas, Base Capture, Hero Vs Villain, among others. You can form duos or trios with your friends for the Arena or play games in teams of up to 15 people depending on the mode.

LEAVE A FEAR

Dominate the Arena and be a legend with your champions. Every battle is a perfect opportunity to show what you are made of, change the course in a team fight and take the victory is in your hands.

PLAY FOR FREE

All champions can be obtained for free in Drainlive, the power has no cost. And they never will.

CONSTANT EVOLUTION

With regular updates, the developers constantly improve and evolve the gameplay, its features and its heroes.
