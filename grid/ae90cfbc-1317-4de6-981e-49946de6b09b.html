Description

The streets of Late City never stop, and neither do you.
Cruise, leap, and fly from car to car through impossible cityscapes to make it to work on time and show your boss that you mean business.

Late City Riders is a single player time trial game where your goal is to make it all the way through the twisting streets and massive jumps of each level to smash in through the window of your office building.
  • Drive, jump, and divekick your way through 20 distinct levels of rapidly escalating difficulty.
  • Jam to a rich and funky soundtrack composed specially for the game
  • Compete against other players around the world, and get the best times on personal and online leaderboards.
  • It's free!

Created by a Funk Fizz, a dev team of students in the University of Utah Entertainment Arts and Engineering program.
