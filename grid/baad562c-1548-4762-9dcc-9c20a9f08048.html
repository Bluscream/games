Description

Battle on the Front Lines with funny, crazy physics. Create your own battles between men and test out everything to the limits.

Game Modes:

  • Sandbox Mode
- Including four maps and possession abilities create your own battles, set your lines, and fight to the limit in Sandbox Mode!!!
- Map types include: Empty Island, Camps Island, Snow Island, and Wild West Island!!!
  • Campaign Mode
- Including 12 levels with a dozen different scenarios test out your strategic abilities in this fun and short piece of FRONT LINES!!!
- Two new historical battle scenarios added into Campaign Mode with update 3.0.

Player Types

Play around with a constantly increasing number of fighter types stretching from ancient times to the modern-day!
