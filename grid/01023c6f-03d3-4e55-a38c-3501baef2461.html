About the Game


*** BETA PERIOD HAS ENDED. WISHLIST THE FULL GAME. THANKS FOR PLAYING AND STAY TUNED FOR THE FULL LAUNCH! ***

https://store.steampowered.com/app/651150/Quantum_League/



As a Quantum Athlete, compete in specially designed arenas where agility, speed and skill are not enough to rise to the top. The ability to tactically play with your past and future selves is key. Quantum League combines muscles with brain in an unique, exciting way to bring online competitive shooters to a new level.

This limited-time Free Open Beta contains a taste of the full Quantum League experience which will release later this year.



The Beta version is limited to the following features:
  • 1v1 mode: Quantum Arena and Overpass arenas
  • 2v2 mode: Quantum Stadium arenas
  • Pick between 4 characters
  • Progress and unlocks will be carried over to the main game once it releases
