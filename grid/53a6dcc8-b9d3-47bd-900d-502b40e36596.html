About the Game

OVERVIEW

Alien-911 Prologue is a free-to-play intro to our hybrid 3rd person shooter and tower defense game. This is our first game and we're excited to bring it to the Steam community.

LIMITATIONS OF PROLOGUE

This prologue version is limited to 3 character classes (out of 4) and 5 maps (out of 22) and caps experience upgades to level 6 (out of 50). The goal is to give players enough visibility into the game so we can gather feedback & suggestions and fix bugs to improve the game before our full release.

KEY FEATURES

  • MULTIPLE CLASSES: 3 unique classes each with 3 abilities, 7 towers, and 4 weapons
  • TACTICAL CONTROL: Keyboard+Mouse and FULL game controller support
  • PLAY WITH YOUR PALS: Play with your best bud in local coop split screen mode
