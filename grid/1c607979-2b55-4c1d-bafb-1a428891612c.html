Description



DESCRIPTION
MOO MOO MOVE is a casual arcade game about guiding chubby cows to safety. Players drive the herd through a host of cute floating islands built with a country feel. UFOs, poachers, ice and more stand between the cows and their next meal - it's down to the player to get them there!

HISTORY
MOO MOO MOVE began as a Tranzfuser project in 2017, where it was awarded funding for its continued development. It is the first game to be released by graduate team MOCHI MODE.

FEATURES
  • Unique hand-painted island levels.
  • A host of hazards to tackle.
  • Cute visuals that make you feel relaxed.
  • 8 round cows just looking for a snack!
