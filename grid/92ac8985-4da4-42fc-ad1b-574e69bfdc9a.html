Description

Globat Pixels is an Open-World game unlike any game you played before! By using OpenStreetMap and precisely interpreting the data, this game enables you to play literally anywhere in the world. Do you want to play next to the Big Ben? In front of the Taj Mahal? Under the Eiffel Tower?
Globat Pixels enables you to do just that. You can also battle your friends or when you don't have any you can still play against bots. In addition, this game offers a great diversity: Besides having an unlimited amount of maps, there's also a big variety of weapons to choose from!
