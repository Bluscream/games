Description


Project Exhibited is a student-made game by Breda University of Applied Sciences.

In Project Exhibited, a thrilling first-person action-stealth game, you play as Dana, the inventor thief. You have been tasked to steal from a private art collection in a mysterious palazzo.

You put your mastery of stealth to the test in a tense mission.
Armed with an arsenal of advanced gadgets, you dodge security systems, outsmart the guards and make some interesting discoveries.
There might be more to these artifacts than meets the eye…


Open-Ended Level Design

We offer freedom of choice with our open-ended level design that allows you to complete the level how you want. Choose your own path through the palazzo and past the guards.

Dynamic Score System

We measure your success through a dynamic score system. Were you aggressive, or super sneaky? Did you disable all security? You can even compete with other players for the high scores!

Intelligent Guards

Our guards and security systems will react to your actions. They cooperate and will scout for you when they see you. Analyze their routines and maybe you can outsmart them.

Unique Gadget System

You are an inventor, so obviously you crafted some gadgets. Use them to outplay the guards, knock them out, distract them, short-circuit electronics and plan your path!
