Description

There was a small miracle which belongs to
the six of us——

Looking for an old friend who went to the battle ground far away,
our heroine, Ligier ,went to the clock tower in secret during wartime to wait for the troop’s coming back.

At there, she saved a young girl named Seena who was been chasing by monsters. Afterwards, together with Michelle, the head of the student council and Ruin ,a mysterious traveler ,they are looking for the way to get out of this school.

However, unexpectedly, they dug out the hidden truth of this school...

The characteristics of this game
▼Adventure RPG with stage form
·A 2D Adventure RPG with simple control and it's completely free！
·Real-time round formed battle！
·All the elements which unnecessarily take your time have been abandoned！

▼A game with simple control and it's completely free！！
Start the game only by tapping it！
No any advertisement or gacha element！
Use magic to beat enemies and clear the game！


▼Real-time round formed battle
Tap the screen at the right time to release a magic shield for reducing damage and preparing for the next attack!
Then use special skill which can turn the game around with one-shot!

▼Without any element which unnecessarily takes your time！
Those elements which cost you a lot of time and energy such as map-moving ,making money or information-collecting are thoroughly abandoned！
Just enjoy the scenario and the adventure！

▼Enjoy the character's training and the battle！
There are six magicians with rich and different characteristics waiting for you to cultivate.
You can strengthen their skills to fight with strong bosses.
The stage you have cleared can be played any number of times！
So you can just enhance these pretty girls level as you want！
